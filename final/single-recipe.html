Banana Pancakes

Dal Baati is a traditional Rajasthani dish consisting of baked dough balls (baati) served with a flavorful lentil curry (dal). Once the dal is cooked to perfection, it is garnished with fresh coriander leaves. This hearty and wholesome dish is often enjoyed with a generous drizzle of ghee on top, adding richness to its flavors. Dal Baati is a comforting and satisfying meal that showcases the rustic flavors of Rajasthan.
